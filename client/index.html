Chess-Like Game

Piece Movement Guide

  • Pawn (P): Moves 1 step in any direction (⬆️ ⬇️ ⬅️ ➡️)
  • Hero1 (H1): Moves 2 steps in any direction (⬆️ ⬇️ ⬅️ ➡️)
  • Hero2 (H2): Moves 2 steps diagonally (↖️ ↗️ ↙️ ↘️)
  • Hero3 (H3): Moves 2 steps in one direction, then 1 step perpendicular (All directions)

Rule of the Game

  • Rule 1 Player has 4 pieces: 1 Pawn, 1 Hero1, 1 Hero2, and 1 Hero3
  • Rule 2 Players take turns moving their pieces
  • Rule 3 First player to reach the opponent's back row wins

Starting the Game

  • Rule 1 Click on a piece A to select it
  • Rule 2 Valid moves will be highlighted in yellow
  • Rule 3 Click on a highlighted box to move the selected piece
